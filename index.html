Lauren's Family Recipes

Cheesecake

This recipe is from my Grandmother and has been a family favorite for many years. My grandmother got this recipe when she worked for a bakery in her early teen years and has been using the same recipe ever since.

Ingredient List:

  • Plain Graham Crakers
  • 24 oz. Plain Cream Cheese
  • 4 Large Eggs
  • 1.75 cups of sugar (divided)
  • 1 stick + 2TB of butter
  • 3TB of vanilla (divided)
