Cheesecake

This recipe is from my grandmother and has been a family favorite for many years. My grandmother got this recipe when she worked for a bakery in her early teen years and has been using the same recipe ever since.

Ingredient List:

  • Plain Graham Crakers
  • 24 oz. Plain Cream Cheese
  • 4 Large Eggs
  • 1.75 cups of sugar (divided)
  • 1 stick + 2TB of butter
  • 3TB of vanilla (divided)
  • Sour Cream

The Process

  1. Melt the butter in the microwave for one minute. The butter shouldn't be hot, but should be almost entirely in liquid form.
  2. Place 30 Graham Cracker Squares into a food processor.
  3. Pour melted butter over Grahma Crackers. Add 1/4 cups of sugar.
  4. Mix until crackers are thoroughly crushed.
  5. Press into 9-inch spring-form pan, going 3/4 up the sides of the pan. Set aside.
  6. After cleaning food processor, blend cream cheese, 1.5 cups sugar, and 3 TB of vanilla. Add eggs and mix.
  7. Pour mixture into crust.
  8. Back at 300 degrees for approximately 1 hr. The center should be a bit wiggly, no more than 3-4 inches in diameter of "wiggly-ness", though.
  9. Allow to cool for 20 minutes.
  10. Mix together sourcream, 2-3 TB of sugar, and 1 tsp of vanilla. Spread on top of the cake.
  11. Bake for 10 minutes longer, then remove and allow to cool completely before placing in refrigerator.
  12. Tastes best if left overnight to cool completely!
